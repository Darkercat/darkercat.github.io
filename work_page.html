Font / Color choice


I am using SF Pro Font and a black color palette because HungryPanda's target group is students. By choosing SF Pro Font and black color will be supported by the iOS app, maintaining its compatibility to the maximum. It is a font that is clear and easy to read, which creates a sense of trust for users. Additionally, I did the accessibility test, providing enough contrast that the content is clearly distinguishable from the surrounding page.
