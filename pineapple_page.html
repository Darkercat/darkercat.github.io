iPhone Sticker

  • A set of iPhone stickers that help students express their rich emotions and their school life at a fast speed.
Project Name Role Tools
iPhone Sticker Graphic Design/Visual Design Adobe XD Illustrator PhotoShop Rotate










Initial Observation


For this project, my goal was to create a new set of pineapple iPhone stickers for students who love fruit. Pineapple Stickers can make iMessage conversations more interactive and students can use fruit to express themselves. It will attract teenagers and adults.



Sketches


My goal is to create a set of stickers that students could express a sentence without typing and also create a set of stickers that students want to use in the group chat when they talk to their peers. So I started observing students’ life, emotions and then I start sketching different emotions.
